THE OFFICIAL AF BIO

Tangents is a 3-piece progressive metal band based out of Bangalore, India. What started out with six optimistic members is now half that number, featuring twice the confusion.

We try to make our music tickle your earlobes whilst you high-five the person next to you in unanimously understood polyrhythms. Nothing satisfies us more than a crowd that satisfies us.
